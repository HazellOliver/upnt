UPNT Patient Portal

Your personalized access to the Urology Partners of North Texas — appointments, test results, and messages all in one secure online space.

About the Portal

The UPNT Patient Portal is a secure web-based platform that empowers patients to take charge of their urological health. From viewing records to scheduling visits, it provides seamless digital access to healthcare.

Key Features

24/7 Access

Log in anytime to review test results, upcoming visits, or your medical summary on desktop or mobile.

Secure Messaging

Privately communicate with your healthcare team for non-urgent queries, prescriptions, or advice.

Appointment Control

Easily book, reschedule, or cancel appointments with real-time updates and reminders.

Easy Billing

Check statements, view insurance details, and pay your bills securely without hassle.

How to Get Started

  1. Go to the official UPNT website and click “Patient Portal”.
  2. Register using your patient ID and email address.
  3. Verify your account and set up your secure password.
  4. Log in and start managing your health online.
