UPNT Patient Portal

Welcome to your secure online space to manage appointments, view test results, and connect with Urology Partners of North Texas — anytime, anywhere.

About the Portal

The UPNT Patient Portal provides a secure and convenient way for patients to stay connected with their healthcare team. Whether you’re accessing lab reports, scheduling appointments, or messaging your provider, the portal simplifies your healthcare experience.

Key Features

24/7 Access

View your records, visit summaries, and test results anytime — from any device.

Secure Messaging

Communicate with your care team for prescriptions or non-urgent queries securely.

Appointment Management

Book, reschedule, or cancel appointments easily with real-time updates.

Billing & Payments

Check invoices, manage insurance, and pay bills online with total security.

How to Get Started

  1. Visit the official UPNT Patient Portal .
  2. Register using your patient ID and email.
  3. Verify your account and create your secure password.
  4. Log in to manage appointments, results, and communication.
